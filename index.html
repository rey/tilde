What I freestyled this evening (it's still in the oven)

  1. Preheat your oven to 220 degrees celcius
  2. Spatchcock your chicken
  3. Crush 4/5 garlic cloves
  4. Grate 3 inches of ginger, squeeze the juice from the pulp into the dish. Chuck away the juiceless ginger pulp.
  5. Add a decent glug of Worcestershire sauce
  6. Add a decent glug of soy sauce
  7. Add a decent glug of olive oil
  8. Throw 5 dots of Tabasco in, too
  9. Season with salt and pepper
  10. Put your chicken in the dish, give it a good mix and turn around so it's covered in the marinade
  11. Put in the fridge for an hour or so
  12. Get your preheated cast iron skillet, throw in a bunch of potatoes and carrots and put your chicken on top
  13. Give it 10/15 minutes at 220 then turn down to 200
  14. Keep checking on it and basting like a boss

How to get passwordless ssh to work on tilde.club

On your machine:

cat .ssh/id_rsa.pub | ssh USER@tilde.club 'cat >> .ssh/authorized_keys'

On tilde:

chmod 700 ~/.ssh && chmod 600 ~/.ssh/authorized_keys
