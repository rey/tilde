How to get passwordless ssh to work on tilde.club

On your machine:

cat .ssh/id_rsa.pub | ssh USER@tilde.club 'cat >> .ssh/authorized_keys'

On tilde:

chmod 700 ~/.ssh && chmod 600 ~/.ssh/authorized_keys
